Pexel values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://www.pexel.com, use our services, or interact with us.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes your personal details such as name, email address, phone number, and other information you provide when filling out forms or contacting us. We also gather data about how you interact with our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. Additionally, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and monitor website performance.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to deliver and improve our services, respond to your inquiries, and communicate with you regarding requested services. It also helps us analyze website usage to improve functionality and user experience. In certain cases, your information may be processed to comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies. Furthermore, we may use your information to personalize your experience on our website, including tailored content and recommendations. We may also use your feedback to improve our service offerings and customer support.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website or delivering services to you. In cases required by law or to protect our rights and safety, we may share information with legal authorities. We may also share aggregated or anonymized data that does not directly identify you for research, analysis, or marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. While we strive to secure your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is entirely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to take steps to protect your personal information, such as using strong passwords and avoiding sharing sensitive information through insecure channels.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of promotional communications or withdraw your consent for data processing where applicable. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below. In addition, you may have the right to lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority if you believe your data has been handled improperly.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies. Any interactions with these third-party websites are subject to their own terms and policies.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@pexel.com. Alternatively, you can reach us at 123 Pexel Street, Cityville, Country or by phone at +1-800-PEXEL-123.
